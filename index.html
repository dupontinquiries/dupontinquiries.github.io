COVID-19
    My Team and I are fighting COVID-19 with The Rocket, a powerful computer.
    We are a team of undergraduate engineering students at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, New York, looking to fight in the battle against COVID-19. Our strategy focuses on high-performance computing, and we need your help! We are joining the Stanford Folding@home (FAH) effort to simulate protein folding, computational drug design, and other types of molecular dynamics. This effort could lead to a new vaccine or treatment for COVID-19. With a powerful computer dedicated to this task, we can help in the fight against this disease and the next one.
